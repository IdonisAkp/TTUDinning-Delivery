Why Choose TTU Dining Delivery?

For Diners

  • Use Your Dining Dollars: Pay with your dining dollars account - no cash needed.
  • Skip the Lines: Avoid long waits at dining halls during peak hours.
  • Convenient Delivery: Food delivered directly to your dorm or study spot.
  • Campus Safe: All deliverers are verified Texas Tech students.

For Deliverers

  • Flexible Schedule: Work whenever you want on your own time.
  • Stay on Campus: All deliveries are short walks around campus.
  • Easy Money: Earn money between classes without leaving TTU.
  • No Formal Shifts: Be your own boss and choose when to work.
